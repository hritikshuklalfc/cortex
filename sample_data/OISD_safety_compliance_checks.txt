OIL INDUSTRY SAFETY DIRECTORATE (OISD) COMPLIANCE CHECKLIST
Refinery Operations & Hot Work Safety Audits
Version: 2026.1

[ ] PERMIT TO WORK (PTW) SYSTEM
    - Are hot work permits issued only after a joint site inspection by the issuer and executor?
    - Is the validity of the permit clearly stated (max 8 hours or single shift)?
    - Are lower explosive limit (LEL) gas tests conducted and recorded at 0% before authorizing welding/cutting?

[ ] FIRE PREVENTION & PROTECTION
    - Are spark arrestors fitted to all diesel-driven equipment operating in Zone 1/Zone 2 hazardous areas?
    - Are fire blankets (minimum 1000°C rating) deployed to cover grating and prevent slag from dropping to lower elevations?
    - Is a dedicated fire watch personnel stationed with a charged fire extinguisher (minimum 9kg DCP)?

[ ] ELECTRICAL SAFETY
    - Are all welding machines properly grounded to a verified earth pit (resistance < 1 ohm)?
    - Are power cables free of joints, cuts, and routed away from walkways to prevent tripping or damage?
    - Is an Earth Leakage Circuit Breaker (ELCB / RCCB) with 30mA sensitivity installed on all temporary power distribution boards?

[ ] CONFINED SPACE ENTRY
    - Is forced ventilation running continuously to ensure oxygen levels remain between 19.5% and 23.5%?
    - Has a rescue plan been formulated, and is rescue equipment (tripod, winch, SCBA) staged at the entry point?
    - Is an entry/exit log maintained by a standby person (hole watch)?
